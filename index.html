HOW IT WORKS

Three Steps.
One Addiction.

01

Step On & Find Your Stance

Stand on the wide EVA deck, adjust your foot position to shoulder width. The board is stable enough to stand on the water before you even start moving.

02

Pump to Generate Lift

Bend your knees and shift weight front-to-back in a rhythmic motion. The high-efficiency wings convert that energy into hydrodynamic lift — the board starts to rise.

03

Fly. Glide. Repeat.

Once you're up, keep pumping to stay airborne. It takes most riders 20–30 minutes to get their first sustained flight — and nobody stops after that.
